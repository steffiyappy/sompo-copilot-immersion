✍ Prompt hygiene for CGA Higienis prompt untuk CGA

  • Start with role.Mulai dengan peran. "I am the PMO Lead / Head of Operations / underwriter at Contoso General Assurance Indonesia". This alone lifts quality."Saya PMO Lead / Head of Operations / underwriter di Contoso General Assurance Indonesia". Ini saja sudah meningkatkan kualitas.
  • Name the audience.Sebut audiens. "steering committee", "regional claims team", "underwriters, 1 to 15 years experience". Copilot calibrates tone accordingly."steering committee", "tim klaim regional", "underwriter, 1 sampai 15 tahun pengalaman". Copilot menyesuaikan nada.
  • Say the language.Sebutkan bahasa. "Bahasa Indonesia formal" or "English, calm and factual". Do not assume."Bahasa Indonesia formal" atau "English, calm and factual". Jangan berasumsi.
  • Set the length.Tetapkan panjang. "300 words", "one page", "12 bullet points". Vague length equals vague output."300 kata", "satu halaman", "12 bullet". Panjang samar berarti output samar.
  • Cite the source.Kutip sumber. "Use the attached file 03_CGA_RAID_Log.xlsx", or "Use publicly available AAJI/AAUI data" if grounding externally."Gunakan berkas terlampir 03_CGA_RAID_Log.xlsx", atau "Gunakan data AAJI/AAUI publik" jika grounding eksternal.
